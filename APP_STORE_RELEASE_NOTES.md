# App Store Release Notes

## Version 1.1

### zh-CN

- 全新优化 Hacker News 信息流，使用更紧凑的卡片布局，分数、来源、评论和收藏状态更清晰。
- 增强多语言阅读体验，支持文章翻译、标题自动翻译和批量标题翻译，减少等待和重复请求。
- 优化详情页、评论区和 AI 总结界面，阅读、讨论和总结流程更统一。
- 修复 Magic Summary 在评论尚未加载完成时可能生成不完整总结的问题。
- 更新默认 AI 配置为 DeepSeek 兼容设置，便于新用户更快完成配置。

### en-US

- Redesigned the Hacker News feed with compact signal cards for clearer scores, sources, comments, and saved states.
- Improved multilingual reading with article translation, automatic title translation, and batched title translation.
- Refined story detail, comments, translated Reader, and Magic Summary screens for a more consistent reading workflow.
- Fixed Magic Summary so discussion summaries wait for comments and include nested replies.
- Updated default AI provider settings to a DeepSeek-compatible configuration for easier setup.
