隐私政策

生效日期： 2026年1月5日

欢迎使用“HNEveryDay”（以下简称“本应用”）。不仅我们致力于为您提供优质的服务，更非常重视您的隐私保护。本《隐私政策》将向您说明我们在您使用服务时如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息，以及您享有的权利。

请您在使用本应用前仔细阅读并确认您已经充分理解本隐私政策所写明的内容。

1. 我们如何收集和使用您的信息

本应用主要通过 HackerNews API 获取公开资讯，并在您的设备上进行展示。我们仅遵循正当、合法、必要的原则收集最少量的必要信息：

  • 网络通讯： 本应用需要访问网络权限以连接 HackerNews 服务器获取最新的信息列表、评论和用户详情。网络请求日志（如IP地址）可能会被 API 提供方（Firebase
  • 本地存储： 本应用可能会在您的设备本地存储您的浏览历史、收藏（书签）等数据，以便为您提供更好的用户体验。这些数据仅存储在您的设备及其关联的 iCloud 账户中，我们不会将其上传至其他服务器。
  • 网页浏览： 当您点击信息链接时，本应用会通过内置浏览器（WebView）加载第三方网页。第三方网站可能会使用 Cookies 或收集您的访问信息，请您参考该第三方网站的隐私政策。

2. 信息的存储

存储地点： 本应用产生的数据（如收藏、设置）主要存储在您的本地设备上。

存储期限： 除非您主动删除或卸载应用，否则本地数据将一直保留。

3. 信息共享、转让和公开披露

我们不会主动共享、转让或公开披露您的个人信息。本应用作为客户端工具，主要连接 Hacker News 公共 API，不存在用户账号注册系统，因此我们不持有您的个人身份信息。

4. 权限说明

本应用申请的权限仅用于提供核心功能：

  • 网络权限 (Internet)： 用于获取新闻数据和加载网页。

本应用不涉及相机、相册、地理位置、日历、麦克风等敏感权限的申请和使用。

5. 未成年人保护

我们非常重视对未成年人个人信息的保护。如果您是未满18周岁的未成年人，在使用本应用前，应在您的父母或其他监护人的监护、指导下共同阅读并同意本隐私政策。

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

Welcome to use "HNEveryDay" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We are committed to providing you with high-quality services and attach great importance to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our services, as well as your rights.

Please read carefully and ensure that you fully understand the content of this Privacy Policy before using the App.

1. How We Collect and Use Your Information

The App primarily fetches public information via the Hacker News API and displays it on your device. We collect minimal necessary information following the principles of legitimacy, lawfulness, and necessity:

  • Network Communication: The App requires network access to connect to Hacker News servers to retrieve the latest stories, comments, and user details. Network request logs (such as IP addresses) may be recorded by the API provider (Firebase/Hacker News), but this is not collected directly by us.
  • Local Storage: The App may store your browsing history, favorites (bookmarks), and other data locally on your device to provide a better user experience. This data is only stored on your device and its associated iCloud account; we do not upload it to any other servers.
  • Web Browsing: When you click on a news link, the App loads third-party webpages via the built-in browser (WebView). Third-party websites may use Cookies or collect your visit information; please refer to the privacy policies of those third-party websites.

2. Data Storage

Storage Location: Data generated by the App (such as favorites, settings) is primarily stored locally on your device.

Storage Period: Local data will remains unless you actively delete it or uninstall the App.

3. Sharing, Transfer, and Public Disclosure

We do not actively share, transfer, or publicly disclose your personal information. As a client tool connecting to the Hacker News public API, the App does not have a user account registration system, and thus we do not hold your personal identity information.

4. Permissions

The permissions requested by the App are only used to provide core functions:

  • Network (Internet): Used to fetch news data and load webpages.

The App does not request or use sensitive permissions such as Camera, Photos, Location, Calendar, or Microphone.

5. Protection of Minors

We attach great importance to the protection of minors' personal information. If you are a minor under the age of 18, you should read and agree to this Privacy Policy under the supervision and guidance of your parents or other guardians before using this App.
