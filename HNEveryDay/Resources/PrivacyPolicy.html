隐私政策

生效日期： 2026年1月5日

欢迎使用“息壤”（HNEveryDay）（以下简称“本应用”）。不仅我们致力于为您提供优质的服务，更非常重视您的隐私保护。本《隐私政策》将向您说明我们在您使用服务时如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息，以及您享有的权利。

请您在使用本应用前仔细阅读并确认您已经充分理解本隐私政策所写明的内容。

1. 我们如何收集和使用您的信息

我们会遵循正当、合法、必要的原则，出于本政策所述的以下目的，收集和使用您在使用服务过程中主动提供或因使用服务而产生的信息：

  • 设备信息： 为了保障软件与服务的安全运行、运营质量及效率，我们会收集您的硬件型号、操作系统版本号、国际移动设备识别码（IMEI）、网络设备硬件地址（MAC）、IP地址等设备信息。
  • 日历权限： 本应用的核心功能涉及日历展示与日程管理。为了向您提供黄历查询及日程服务，我们需要请求访问您的日历权限。我们仅在本地读取和写入日历数据，不会将其上传至任何云端服务器。
  • 网络权限： 本应用需要网络权限以获取实时的市场行情数据（如股票指数、汇率等）和进行必要的数据更新。

2. 信息的存储

存储地点： 我们依照法律法规的规定，将境内收集的个人信息存储于中华人民共和国境内。

存储期限： 我们仅在为提供服务之目的所必需的期间内保留您的个人信息，超出必要期限后，我们将对您的个人信息进行删除或匿名化处理，但法律法规另有规定的除外。

3. 信息共享、转让和公开披露

我们不会与任何公司、组织和个人共享您的个人信息，但以下情况除外：

  • 事先获得您的明确授权或同意；
  • 根据适用的法律法规、法律程序的要求、强制性的行政或司法要求所必须的情况下进行提供；

我们不会将您的个人信息转让给任何公司、组织和个人，也不会公开披露您的个人信息。

4. 您的权利

按照中国相关的法律、法规、标准，我们保障您对自己的个人信息行使以下权利：

  • 访问和更正： 您有权通过应用内的设置访问和更正您的信息。
  • 删除： 在某些情形下，您可以向我们提出删除个人信息的请求。
  • 撤回同意： 您可以通过设备系统设置撤回各项权限的授权（如日历权限）。

5. 未成年人保护

我们非常重视对未成年人个人信息的保护。如果您是未满18周岁的未成年人，在使用本应用前，应在您的父母或其他监护人的监护、指导下共同阅读并同意本隐私政策。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问、意见或建议，请通过以下方式与我们联系：

电子邮箱：me@corlin.cn

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

Welcome to use "HNEveryDay" (XiRang) (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We are committed to providing you with high-quality services and attach great importance to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our services, as well as your rights.

Please read carefully and ensure that you fully understand the content of this Privacy Policy before using the App.

1. How We Collect and Use Your Information

We collect and use information that you actively provide or that is generated during your use of the service, following the principles of legitimacy, lawfulness, and necessity, for the following purposes:

  • Device Information: To ensure the secure operation, operational quality, and efficiency of the software and services, we may collect your hardware model, operating system version, International Mobile Equipment Identity (IMEI), MAC address, IP address, and other device information.
  • Calendar Access: The core function of the App involves calendar display and schedule management. To provide you with almanac queries and scheduling services, we need to request access to your calendar. We only read and write calendar data locally and do not upload it to any cloud servers.
  • Network Access: The App requires network access to obtain real-time market data (such as stock indices, exchange rates, etc.) and perform necessary data updates.

2. Data Storage

Storage Location: In accordance with laws and regulations, we store personal information collected within China in the People's Republic of China.

Storage Period: We retain your personal information only for the period necessary to provide the service. After the necessary period, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, unless otherwise provided by laws and regulations.

3. Sharing, Transfer, and Public Disclosure

We will not share your personal information with any company, organization, or individual, except in the following cases:

  • With your explicit authorization or consent;
  • As required by applicable laws and regulations, legal procedures, or mandatory administrative or judicial requirements.

We will not transfer your personal information to any company, organization, or individual, nor will we publicly disclose your personal information.

4. Your Rights

In accordance with relevant Chinese laws, regulations, and standards, we safeguard your rights to your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your information through the settings in the App.
  • Deletion: In certain circumstances, you can request us to delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw authorization for various permissions (such as calendar access) through your device system settings.

5. Protection of Minors

We attach great importance to the protection of minors' personal information. If you are a minor under the age of 18, you should read and agree to this Privacy Policy under the supervision and guidance of your parents or other guardians before using this App.
